This is a lightweight tool that creates a histogram of a NFL player's yards per attempt over the course of a season.

To use, select a player from the top right of the screen, then hit go.

Known bug: historical information for players who switched teams this year (for example LeSean McCoy, DeMarco Murray) doesn't work. Sorry :/
